  • How spread betting works

    In essence, spread betting is very simple. Will the market go up or down? There is a buy and sell price. This is known as the spread. Think the market will rise? 'Go long' and enter a position at the higher price, the buy price. Think it will fall? 'Go short' and enter a position at the lower, or sell price.

New to Spread Betting?

Spread Betting allows you to speculate on markets without having to buy the underlying assets. It has a number of advantages over traditional share dealing:

  • Exemption from Capital Gains Tax (link to t&c saying law may be different for you in your country or where you have residence).

  • Ability to leverage trades

  • Make a profit when share prices fall as well as rise

  • Pay no commission

Before you start trading, do your research into both historical and current market movements.
